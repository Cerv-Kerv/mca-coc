Use this service to apply for the following Certificates of Competency (CoC) for deck:

Officer of the Watch (OOW)

  • - Unlimited II/1
  • - Less than 500gt Near Coastal II/3
  • - Less than 500gt limited to Category D waters II/3

Chief Mate

  • Unlimited All Routes
  • Less than 3,000 GT, unlimited area
  • Less than 3,000 GT, near-coastal
  • Unlimited tonnage, near coastal

Master

  • Unlimited
  • Less than 3,000 GT, unlimited area
  • Unlimited Tonnage, near-coastal
  • Less than 500 GT, near coastal including German waters
  • Less than 500gt, limited to Category D waters
  • Less than 3,000 GT, Near Coastal, specified trading areas

For all other applications, refer to the related guidance (opens in a new window).

What you will need

To access this service you will need to provide your:

  • date of birth
  • Seafarer Reference Number (SRN)

To complete your application you will need to provide:

  • evidence of your qualifying seagoing service
  • information to support your application

What your application costs

A new application costs £220.

Applications for reapplying or upgrading vary in cost. Exact costs will be confirmed before you complete your application.

Before you start

If you do not have a Seafarer Reference Number (SRN)

Your SRN may also be known to you as a Seafarer Documentation System (SDS) number.

If you do not have a SDS or SRN, you will need to request a SRN. We will require an image of your valid passport to verify your identity. Requesting a SRN takes around 5 minutes.

If you cannot find your Seafarer Reference Number (SRN)

If you cannot find your SRN, email, deck@mcga.gov.uk

Other ways you can apply

You can apply by post.
