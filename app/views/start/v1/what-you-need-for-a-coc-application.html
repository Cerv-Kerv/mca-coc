Information you may need to provide for your Certificate of Competency (CoC) application

We have provided the information required for a deck Certificate of Competency (CoC). The exact requirements will vary for specific applications.

Notice of Eligibility (NOE)

Identity and medical

  • - Passport-style photo
  • - Medical fitness certificate

Training and education

  • - College education and training enrolment letter
  • - Personal Survival Techniques (STCW A-VI/1-1)
  • - Fire Prevention and Fire Fighting (STCW A-VI/1-2)
  • - Elementary First Aid (STCW A-VI/1-3)
  • - Personal Safety and Social Responsibility (STCW A-VI/1-4)

Seagoing service

  • - Qualifying seagoing service
  • - Seagoing service evidence
  • - Training Record Book
  • - Workbooks
  • - Specified Area

Other requirements

  • - SMarT funded status
  • - Return-delivery address

Supporting evidence

Training and education

  • - College education and training completion letter with exemptions
  • - OOW Unlimited level college education and training completion letter
  • - College education and training completion letter
  • - Navigation, and Stability and Operations MCA-approved written exams
  • - General Ship Knowledge, Chart Work, and Practical Navigation MCA-approved written exams
  • - Navigation, and Stability and Structure MCA-approved written exams
  • - Magnetic Compass and Navigational Aids, Ship Construction and Stability, and Shipboard Operations MCA-approved written exams
  • - Proficiency in Survival Craft and Rescue Boats (STCW A-VI/2-1)
  • - Advanced Fire Fighting (STCW A-VI/3)
  • - Medical First Aid (STCW A-VI/4-1)
  • - Medical Care Certificate (STCW A-VI/4-2)

Ancillary certificates

  • - Signals
  • - Efficient Deck Hand
  • - NARAS/NAEST Operational (ECDIS if NAEST completed before June 2005)
  • - NARAS/NAEST Management
  • - HELM Operational
  • - HELM Management

Other requirements

  • - GMDSS
  • - Signature
  • - Delivery address

What you need to do next

Go to the Apply for and manage seafarer certificates service to start your application.
