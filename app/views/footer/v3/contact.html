Contact us

If you need help with this service or have a question, contact the relevant team using the provided information.

When emailing us, include your full name, your Seafarer Reference Number (SRN), and a brief description of your query.

Helpdesk

For technical queries, general enquiries, or for help with accessing the service, you can:

  • Email: seafareroperations@mcga.gov.uk
  • Phone: +44 (0)333 399 0007
  • Hours: 8am to 4pm

Certification queries

For queries relating to your Notice of Eligibility (NoE), Seafarer Reference Number (SRN) certifications, eligibility, or documentation, you can:

  • Email: deck@mcga.gov.uk (for deck officers)
  • Email: engineering@mcga.gov.uk (for engineering officers)
  • Phone: +44 (0)333 399 0040
  • Hours: 08:30am to 5pm

Oral Exam queries

For queries relating to your exam booking, you can:

  • Email: onlineoralexams@mcga.gov.uk

Exam (Part A) and appeal queries

For exam (Part A), submission of evidence, or appeals queries you can:

  • Email: exams@mcga.gov.uk
