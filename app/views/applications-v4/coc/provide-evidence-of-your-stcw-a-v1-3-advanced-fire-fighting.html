Training and education

Provide evidence of your STCW A-V1/3 Advanced Fire Fighting

We require evidence of your STCW A-V1/3 Advanced Fire Fighting training to support your application.

The Maritime and Coastguard Agency (MCA) will accept certificates issued under the authority of any EU Member State, as well as by any country noted in MIN 643 (opens in a new window).

If your certificate is more than 5 years old, provide the original certificate along with your current, in date, refresher certificate.
