Other requirements

Provide your signature

We need your signature to use within your application. The signature you provide will be used on documents issued to you.

We’ve provided an image of a sample Certificate of Competency (CoC) card. This image shows you where your signature will be located on the card.

The signature you provide for us to use within your application must be:

  • less than 8.5cm by 5.4cm
  • written using a black ballpoint pen on plain white paper

The signature upload that you provide must:

  • match the signature on your passport
  • be legible

The signature upload that you provide must match the signature on your passport and must be legible.
