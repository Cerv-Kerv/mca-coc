Other requirements

Provide your signature

The Maritime and Coastguard Agency (MCA) need your signature to use within your application. The signature you provide will be used on documents issued to you.

We’ve provided an image of a sample Certificate of Competency (CoC) card. This image shows you where your signature will be located on the card.

The signature you provide must:

  • be less than 5.5cm by 0.72cm
  • be written using a black ballpoint pen on plain white paper
  • match the signature on your passport
  • be legible
