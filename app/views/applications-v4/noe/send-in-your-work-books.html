Send us your supporting workbooks

Your workbook is the book you use to note the detail of the tasks in your Training Record Book (TRB).

Sending in your workbooks

Write your Seafarer Reference Number (SRN) on a piece of paper and attach it to your workbooks before posting them to us. We will pair them with the related application you have submitted.

Your original workbooks must be posted to our office as a hard copy. Workbooks cannot be uploaded.
