Seagoing service

Send your Training Record Book (TRB) to the Maritime and Coastguard Agency (MCA)

Your training record book should list all performed activities, signed off by officers whilst at sea. All recorded training acts as evidence that will support your application.

Ensure your training book is complete. An incomplete training book can delay the procesing of your application or mean that it's not approved.

Sending in your Training Record Book

Write your Seafarer Reference Number (SRN) on a piece of paper and attach it to your TRB before posting it to us. We will pair it with the related application that you have submitted.

Your original Training Record Book (TRB) must be posted sent to our office as a hard copy. Workbooks cannot be uploaded.
